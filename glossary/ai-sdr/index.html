Glossary

What is an AI SDR?

An AI SDR is an AI agent that does sales development work: holding sales conversations, qualifying leads, and booking meetings. The category splits in two: outbound AI SDRs that prospect cold, and AI inbound SDRs that capture, qualify, and book the leads a business already has.

The split that matters

"AI SDR" is an umbrella label, and the umbrella hides the most important buying decision. The two motions underneath it share almost nothing:

Outbound AI SDRAI inbound SDR
Starts withA list and a target accountA lead that already raised a hand
ChannelsCold email, social touches, dialersWeb form, email, chat, and phone
Core jobRelevance at volumeSpeed, qualification, and routing
Failure modeSpam at scale, burned domainsA real buyer fumbled or ignored
Success metricMeetings sourcedQualified meetings from existing demand

The full definitional treatment of the inbound side lives in the AI inbound SDR entry and the complete inbound SDR guide.

Why it matters

The category crossed from experiment to product: Gartner's 2025 research note describes prebuilt AI SDR agents as commercially available and viable for repetitive selling tasks at scale (Gartner, Innovation Insight: AI SDR Agents).

The strongest case is inbound, because the inbound job is the one humans physically cannot do: instant response on every channel at every hour. Audits keep finding the gap; a 2024 audit of 1,000 B2B SaaS websites found 63.5% of companies never responded to a demo request at all.

The buying mistake to avoid: evaluating an "AI SDR" without asking which motion it was built for. A cold-outreach engine bolted onto your inbound queue mishandles hand-raisers; an inbound qualifier pointed at cold lists has nothing to qualify.

Common questions

Do AI SDRs actually work?

For repetitive sales development tasks, yes: Gartner's 2025 research note describes prebuilt AI SDR agents as commercially available and viable at scale. Results depend on the motion. Inbound triage is the strongest case, because speed and consistency are the whole job. Cold outbound is harder and riskier.

Do I need an inbound or an outbound AI SDR?

If the problem is empty pipeline, outbound. If the problem is leads arriving faster than your team can respond and qualify honestly, inbound. The failure modes are opposite, so a tool built for one motion is rarely good at the other. The comparison: inbound SDR vs outbound SDR.

Is an AI SDR the same as a chatbot?

No. A chatbot answers questions on one channel with scripted flows. An AI SDR makes qualification decisions, writes to the CRM, and books meetings. The full breakdown: AI inbound SDR vs chatbot.
