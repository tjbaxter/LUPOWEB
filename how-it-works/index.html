Filtering

Stop the noise from reaching sales.

Four kinds of noise. Gone before sales ever sees them.

Filtered

Vendor pitches

Other companies selling in. Declined, logged, never shown to sales.

Routed

Support leakage

Existing-customer issues in the wrong queue. Routed to the right team, in context.

Followed up

Half-engaged buyers

Started a chat or a form, then vanished. Chased through their own channel until they qualify or close out.

Disqualified

Wrong-fit prospects

Real interest, wrong fit. Handled without burning an AE's time.

Enrichment & scoring

Tell reps who's actually ready.

Qualified isn't ready to buy. LUPO surfaces the intent signals reps would otherwise miss.

Enriched

Company context, automatic

Headcount, revenue, tech stack, decision-maker title. Appended before a rep opens the record.

Scored

Buying-intent signals

Funding, M&A, leadership hires, headcount growth. Surfaced per lead, so reps know who's in-market.

Ranked

Today's hottest, on top

Ranked by intent score, not arrival time. Same-day calls rise to the top.

Surfaced
