How LUPO works

Capture, filter, enrich, score, route.

One agentic brain across phone, email, web form, and chat. Every inbound gets the same treatment, in the same context, before it ever reaches a rep.

Filtering

Stop the noise from reaching sales.

Vendor pitches, support requests, half-engaged visitors, wrong-fit prospects. All handled before sales sees them.

Filtered

Vendor pitches

Other companies selling into your demo forms, phone lines, and chatbot. Politely declined, logged, and never routed to sales.

Routed

Support leakage

Existing customers and account issues arriving through sales channels. Routed to the right team with the original conversation attached.

Followed up

Half-engaged buyers

Visitors who start a chat, submit a form, or reply once and disappear. Followed up through the channel they used until qualified or closed out.

Disqualified

Wrong-fit prospects

Real interest, wrong company size, use case, geography, or timing. Handled appropriately without taking AE time.

Enrichment & scoring

Tell reps who's actually ready.

Qualified is not the same as ready to buy. LUPO enriches every inbound and surfaces the buying-intent signals reps would otherwise miss.

Enriched

Company context, automatic

Headcount, industry, revenue band, tech stack, and decision-maker title, appended to every inbound, before a rep opens the record.

Scored

Buying-intent signals

Recent funding, M&A, leadership hires, headcount growth, and category-specific triggers surfaced per lead. Reps know who's actually in-market.

Ranked

Today's hottest, on top

Inbound ranked by buying-intent score, not arrival time. The leads worth a same-day call rise to the top of the queue.

Surfaced

Triggers your reps would miss

A lead's company announced a Series B yesterday. The VP Sales joined three weeks ago. Reps see the signal in Slack before they pick up the phone.

One sales policy. Every inbound channel.

A different response for each channel.

Not every form is a demo request. LUPO matches the response to the channel based on your policy.

Inbound phone

Answers live, qualifies, books, escalates.

Picks up on the second ring, qualifies the caller against your policy, captures buying intent, checks live availability, books real appointments, and escalates to a human when the policy says so.

Web forms

Classifies before it reacts.

Classifies the submission, enriches context where it can, and decides whether to email, call back, post to Slack, book a meeting, or hold the lead for human approval. No blanket callbacks to senior buyers.

Chat Qualification

Your chatbot opens the door. LUPO closes the lead.

Takes intent and consent from your existing chatbot (Intercom Fin, Drift, HubSpot Chat, or a custom widget like ours here), continues qualification, and books inside the same conversation when the buyer is ready.

Agentic Email Qualification

Cadenced follow-up until a meeting or a clean no.

For leads that need email rather than an immediate call. Per-org sender domains, audience filters, and reply handling are tuned to your stack.
