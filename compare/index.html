The category is consolidating onto Salesforce.

The inbound conversational layer is being pulled onto the Salesforce platform. Qualified (and its agent Piper) completed its acquisition by Salesforce on 1 April 2026, folding into Agentforce. Salesforce then signed a definitive agreement to acquire Fin (formerly Intercom) on 15 June 2026 (expected to close in a later fiscal quarter, not yet closed), also bound for Agentforce. Drift, the category's pioneer, has been winding down since its Salesloft acquisition. If you run HubSpot, the leading inbound agents are becoming Salesforce-platform products. LUPO is the independent, HubSpot-native inbound SDR built for your stack.
