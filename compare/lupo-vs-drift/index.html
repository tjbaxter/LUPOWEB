LUPO vs Drift

Drift pioneered conversational marketing. Salesloft acquired it in 2024, and a gradual sunset was publicly reported in March 2026. LUPO is the AI inbound SDR: web form, email, chat, and phone. Here is the honest comparison.

The short answer

Choose LUPO if you want a successor that covers more than website chat. LUPO runs web form, email, chat, and phone with one agentic brain. It filters noise, enriches every lead through proprietary data partnerships, scores buying intent, and books real buyers. Pricing is 3,000 to 15,000 USD per month, stated upfront, starting with a 30-day founder-led pilot, fully live in 4 to 8 weeks.

Choose Drift if you are already in the Salesloft and Clari ecosystem, your inbound runs on website chat, and the publicly reported transition to 1mind suits your roadmap.

Where Drift stands in 2026

Many good teams built their inbound motion on Drift. It defined conversational marketing, and it earned its installed base. The question now is succession. Salesloft acquired Drift in February 2024. In March 2026, Clari and Salesloft announced a gradual sunset of Drift, with 1mind named as its successor, as publicly reported. Renewal minimums are publicly reported around 30,000 USD per year. In September 2025, an OAuth integration breach affected hundreds of organizations, as documented by Forrester.

For teams choosing what comes next, the useful question is scope. Website chat catches the visitors who click the bubble. It never touched the buyer who calls, the demo form submitted at 11pm, or the inbound email. A successor can cover all of it. That is the case LUPO makes: one agentic brain across web form, email, chat, and phone, with every lead enriched and scored before a rep sees it. See how it works.

If your stack runs on Salesforce, the natural shortlist also includes Piper: see LUPO vs Qualified. If the conversations on your site are mostly support, see LUPO vs Intercom Fin. The wider field is mapped in Qualified Alternatives for HubSpot Teams.

Common questions

Is Drift being discontinued?

In March 2026, Clari and Salesloft announced a gradual sunset of Drift, with 1mind named as its successor, as publicly reported. Existing contracts continue through the transition. Confirm current status with Salesloft before making decisions.

Can LUPO take over from Drift's website chat?
