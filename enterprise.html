Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LUPO Enterprise

Why a pilot instead of a free trial?
Pilots create real adoption and outcomes. Free trials rarely get reps using the product. To keep it risk-free, we offer a 7-day full refund.
Can we start with more than 5 seats?
Yes. 5 seats ($1,000/mo) is the minimum. Start with 10, 20, or more at $200/seat/month.
What happens if we add seats mid-month?
Stripe automatically prorates the billing. You only pay for the remaining days in the current billing cycle for new seats.
Does LUPO support compliance-heavy industries?
Today we prevent risky promises and unauthorized discounts. Deeper rulebook integrations (insurance, pharma, finance) are coming via the LUPO Compliance Engine.
