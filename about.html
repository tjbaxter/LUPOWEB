About LUPO

A customer fills out your web form at 9:47 PM. By 9:48 PM, they are on a call with LUPO. By 9:50 PM, they are booked into your calendar. Your team sees the deal in the morning. Qualified, scheduled, ready to go.

LUPO is the AI sales agent that answers every inbound lead the second it lands.

When a customer calls you, fills out your web form, or asks for a callback through your chatbot, LUPO picks up instantly. It qualifies the enquiry, answers from your approved business information, checks your calendar in real time, and books the appointment.

Most businesses don't lose customers because they lack demand. They lose them because response is too slow. A missed call, an ignored web form, a delayed chatbot follow-up, and the customer books with the next business instead. LUPO is built to stop that happening.

It is not a generic AI receptionist. LUPO is an agent. It makes its own decisions in real time about which question to ask, when to check your calendar, when to book, when to escalate to a human, and when to close the call. It's the inbound salesperson that never goes home.

Not every enquiry deserves a sales call

LUPO qualifies inbound leads before they reach your team. It tells the difference between real buyers asking about your services, low-intent enquiries, vendors trying to sell to you, support requests, wrong-fit customers, and urgent opportunities that need immediate follow-up.

Qualified leads get booked or routed to the right person on your team. Everything else is logged so your reps don't waste their time.

For larger teams the problem isn't only missed response. It is wasted sales capacity. Web forms, customer portals, and chat widgets attract fake leads, vendors, support requests, and poorly qualified enquiries. LUPO is the first-pass qualification layer that filters and routes inbound demand before it ever reaches sales.

What LUPO does

Inbound calls

24/7 answering, qualification, pricing guidance, availability checks, and booking.

Web form responses

Instant reply on the right channel. For consumer enquiries, LUPO calls back inside sixty seconds. For senior B2B buyers, LUPO sends a personal email with a one-click booking link. No surprise robot calls to VPs.

Chat handoffs

Callbacks triggered when your existing chatbot captures a phone number and consent.

Calendar booking

Live availability and real appointment creation. Google Calendar today, Outlook coming in your onboarding window.

CRM sync

Every contact, call summary, and booking written into HubSpot automatically. Salesforce next.

Slack alerts

Your team sees qualified leads and bookings the second they happen.

Lead routing

Round-robin or rules-based handoff to the right rep on your team.

Full call logging

Transcripts, recordings, outcomes, and a clean audit trail for every interaction.
