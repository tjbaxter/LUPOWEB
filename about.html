About LUPO

A buyer fills out your web form at 9:47 PM. By 9:48 PM, LUPO has qualified the lead, scored buying intent from live company signals, and booked the meeting into your rep's calendar. Your team sees a ready-to-go deal in the morning. Nothing leaked. No rep time burned.

LUPO is the AI inbound SDR. Capture to booked meeting, fully automated.

The moment an inbound lead lands, on phone, email, web form, or chat, LUPO picks it up, qualifies the enquiry against your definition of qualified, scores real buying intent, and books the meeting straight into your reps' calendars. Speed and qualification at the same time.

It is not another routing tool. LUPO is an agent. It decides in real time which question to ask, when to check your calendar, when to book, when to escalate to a human, and when to close the conversation. It is the inbound salesperson that never goes home.

Reps drowning in unqualified inbound is wasted sales capacity

For mid-market and enterprise sales teams the inbound problem is not missed calls. It is what reaches reps in the first place. Web forms, customer portals, and chat widgets attract fake leads, vendors, support requests, and poorly qualified enquiries. Every one of those that lands on a rep's calendar is paid sales capacity burned on noise.

LUPO is the first-pass qualification layer that filters and routes inbound demand before it ever reaches sales. It tells the difference between real buyers, low-intent enquiries, vendors selling in, support requests, wrong-fit prospects, and urgent opportunities that need immediate follow-up.

Qualified buyers get booked or routed to the right rep. Everything else is logged so your team never sees it.

What LUPO does

Buying-intent scoring

Every inbound lead is enriched with live company-signal data. LUPO pulls funding rounds, M&A, leadership hires, headcount growth, and scores real buying intent against your own definition of in-market. Your reps see the score and the reasons, not a black box.

Inbound calls

24/7 answering, qualification, pricing guidance, availability checks, and booking.

Web form responses

Instant reply on the right channel. A demo form gets a personal email with a one-click booking link in minutes. An inbound call gets answered live. A chat continues in chat. LUPO only dials a lead who has asked to be called.

Chat

LUPO's chat widget qualifies in the conversation, with voice dictation, file uploads, and booking built in. It hands off to a human the moment one is needed.

Agentic Email Qualification

Inbound email enquiries read, qualified, and answered with a personal reply and a one-click booking link. Cadenced follow-up until a meeting or a clean no.

Calendar booking

Live availability and real appointment creation across Google Calendar and Microsoft 365 / Outlook.

CRM sync

Every contact, call summary, and booking written to your CRM. HubSpot and Salesforce are live. Additional connectors light up on request.

Alerts and routing

Your team sees qualified leads the second they happen. Round-robin or rules-based handoff to the right rep.

Full audit trail

Transcripts, recordings, outcomes, and the reasoning behind every decision LUPO made on every interaction.

Why LUPO

Inbound SDR time is the most expensive resource in a sales org and the easiest to burn. Reps spend their days triaging vendor pitches, support requests in the wrong queue, wrong-fit prospects, and half-engaged form fills while real buyers wait. The form-and-chat routing tools that run most sales orgs today sit on top of submitted forms and route whatever they are given. None of them qualify, and none of them touch voice.

LUPO is the qualification layer in front of the routing layer. It answers the inbound, qualifies the conversation, scores buying intent from live company signals, and either books the real buyer or drops the noise before it ever reaches a rep.
