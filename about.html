About LUPO

LUPO was built to solve a problem every professional service business knows too well: missed client calls turning into lost opportunities.

Across dozens of conversations with service firms, the same issue came up repeatedly. When calls come in during meetings, client visits, or outside business hours, they go unanswered. In time-sensitive professional services, that delay is decisive. The caller simply moves on to the next firm who picks up.

Unlike passive lead generation, professional services operate on immediate availability. A missed call isn't a follow-up opportunity. It's a client decision made within minutes.

Traditional solutions fall short. Answering services take messages. IVRs frustrate callers. Receptionists can't assess urgency, qualification criteria, or routing decisions in real time. By the time someone calls back, the client has already made a choice.

LUPO was built specifically for time-sensitive professional service workflows.

It handles the decisions that matter:

  • Is this an urgent request or a scheduled inquiry?
  • Is this within your service area or expertise?
  • What type of service is needed?
  • Should this trigger immediate response or scheduling?
  • Who needs to be notified right now?

LUPO is not a generic AI receptionist. It is purpose-built for professional service firms and understands the difference between routine inquiries and time-critical situations requiring immediate action.

How LUPO works

LUPO answers every inbound call 24/7, qualifies the situation in real time, then turns it into an actionable notification for your team.

For urgent requests, LUPO captures the key details and contact information, tags the inquiry type, and instantly notifies your on-call team with priority details so someone can respond immediately.

For scheduled requests, LUPO captures the preferred day/time and sends it as an appointment request so your team can confirm or propose an alternative.

Every call is logged with the transcript, call recording, and the exact notification that was sent.

Availability

LUPO is live and currently handling client calls for professional service firms across the United States. Capacity is intentionally limited to ensure reliable performance and direct support during onboarding.
