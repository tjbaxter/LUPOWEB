Our Story

Founded by Cambridge Alumni

LUPO was founded by Cambridge University alumni to help technical students land their dream jobs. We saw the bottleneck of incredibly intelligent AI being confined to chat browser interfaces.

Our vision: break AI out of the browser with ultra-low latency streaming invocation for ultra-fast AI answers using state-of-the-art speech-to-text APIs.

What started as a tool for technical interviews has expanded to everyday use cases and real-time assistance for sales teams. The fastest way on your laptop to find out anything.
