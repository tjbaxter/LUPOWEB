Common Questions

Everything you need to know about LUPO

Setup & Onboarding

How long does setup take?
3 minutes. Upload your company info (or paste/speak it), add your name and what you sell, and you're ready. Works with any video call tool, Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, etc. No integrations needed.

During Calls

Does LUPO work with my video call software?
Yes. LUPO works with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and any other video platform/dialer. It's a desktop app that captures your system audio, so there's nothing to install in your video software. LUPO also integrates with dialers. Just start your call and LUPO listens.
Can prospects see that I'm using LUPO?
No. LUPO appears only on your screen, it's like having notes open during a call. Prospects can't see it. You should still follow your company's call recording and consent policies.
What if I don't know the industry terms prospects use?
That's exactly what live translation is for. When prospects say "120% NRR" or "API rate limits," LUPO instantly shows you what it means in plain English. Works for any industry, finance, tech, healthcare, manufacturing, etc.
How fast are the responses during calls?
0.5-1 seconds. When an objection is detected or you type "what should I say?", LUPO responds in 0.5-1 seconds with tactical suggestions based on your company data, not generic advice.

Post-Call

How does deep research work?
After every call, click "Deep Research" and LUPO analyzes the full transcript, researches the prospect's company (news, funding, competitors), and generates a tactical playbook with exact follow-up phrases, competitive positioning, and intelligence to weaponize. Takes seconds, saves hours.
Can I use LUPO's summaries in my CRM?
Yes. Post-call summaries, next steps, and emails are all copy-paste ready. Generate in seconds, then paste directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or wherever you log calls.

Data & Privacy

Is my call data secure?
Yes. Audio is encrypted in transit and processed on our secure servers. We're SOC 2 aligned and GDPR compliant. Your call transcripts and company data are never used to train AI models or shared with third parties. You can delete any call data at any time.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored on secure, encrypted cloud servers. You have full control, delete any call transcript or report whenever you want. We never sell or share your data.
Does LUPO record my calls?
LUPO transcribes your calls in real-time to provide guidance, summaries, and research. You control what's saved and can delete transcripts anytime. Always follow your company's recording and consent policies.

Use Cases

Does this work for all industries?
Yes. LUPO translates terminology from any industry, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, tech, real estate, insurance, etc. Whether prospects say "alpha generation" or "OEE optimization," LUPO explains it instantly.
Is this just for SDRs or can AEs use it too?
Both. SDRs use it for cold calls and discovery. AEs use it for demos and closing calls. Anyone on a sales call benefits, new reps learn faster, experienced reps close more deals.
What if I sell to multiple industries?
LUPO adapts. Set your call objective before each call ("calling healthcare CIO" or "fintech CFO") and LUPO tailors responses to that context. Works across any vertical you sell into.

Manager Questions

Can managers see their team's calls?
Yes. Managers get an overview of how their teams use LUPO, not individual micro managing. Use it for coaching, pipeline visibility, and spotting common objections across the team.
How does this help onboard new reps?
New reps get real-time coaching from day one. They see translations of unfamiliar terms, get objection handling suggestions, and learn what to say next, without waiting for manager feedback. Cuts ramp time significantly.

Technical

What if my internet is slow during a call?
LUPO is lightweight and works on most connections. If your internet can handle a video call, it can handle LUPO. Responses are optimized for speed, 0.5-1 seconds even on slower connections.
Does LUPO slow down my computer?
No. LUPO is optimized to use minimal CPU and memory. It runs quietly in the background and won't affect your video call quality or other applications.
