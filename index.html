How does LUPO work?
LUPO listens to your live calls, detects key phrases, and provides instant on-screen guidance — all in under 200ms. No special setup required.

No. LUPO performs analysis in real time and does not retain audio or transcripts after your call ends. Your conversations remain private.

No — just install the desktop app for Mac or Windows. It runs quietly in the background and overlays on any call tool (Zoom, Teams, Meet, etc.).
