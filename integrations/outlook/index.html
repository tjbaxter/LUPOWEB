LUPO + Outlook

LUPO books real meetings on Microsoft 365 / Outlook calendars, live today, with the same live availability checks and event creation as Google Calendar.

What it does

Microsoft-first teams get the full booking flow. LUPO qualifies the lead on web form, email, chat, or phone, then turns the conversation into a real meeting.

CapabilityHow it works
AvailabilityLUPO checks live availability in the rep's Microsoft 365 / Outlook calendar before offering any time
BookingA real event is created on the calendar, live today, not a request waiting for someone to confirm
RoutingThe meeting lands on the right rep's calendar, with routing rules configured during onboarding
ContextThe booking arrives with context attached, and the full record is written to your CRM

How it works for your team

A buyer emails your sales inbox on Saturday morning. LUPO replies, qualifies them, checks the rep's Outlook calendar live, and offers slots that are actually free. The buyer picks one and the event is on the calendar before Monday standup.

The rep opens the invite and the context is attached. The full record, including the conversation summary, transcript link, and intent score, is in the CRM. They walk into the meeting prepared, with zero scheduling back-and-forth.

Setup

Microsoft 365 / Outlook is connected during pilot onboarding, OAuth-based, with rep routing configured hand-in-hand with your team to match how you already work. Booking is live inside the 4 to 8 week rollout. See how it works and pricing, or browse all integrations including Google Calendar and Salesforce.

Common questions

Is Outlook booking live today?

Yes. Microsoft 365 / Outlook booking is live today, with the same live availability checks and real event creation as Google Calendar.

Which calendar does the meeting land on?

The right rep's calendar, with context attached. Routing rules are configured during onboarding to match how your team works.

Can LUPO double-book a rep on Outlook?

No. LUPO checks live availability before offering any time, so it only offers slots that are actually free, and the booking is a real event on the calendar.
