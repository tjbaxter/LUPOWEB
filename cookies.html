Cookie Policy

Learn how LUPO uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and protect your privacy.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our applications. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing how you use our service, and ensuring our platform works securely and efficiently.

LUPO takes a privacy-first approach to cookies. We only use cookies that are necessary for our service to function or that you have explicitly consented to. We never sell cookie data to third parties or use it for intrusive advertising.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website and application to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, authentication, and basic navigation.

  • Authentication tokens to keep you logged in
  • Security cookies to protect against attacks
  • Session management for application state
  • Load balancing for optimal performance

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences and settings. You can opt out, but some features may not work as expected.

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Language and region settings
  • UI customization choices
  • Feature usage preferences

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use LUPO so we can improve our service. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

  • Page views and user journeys
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error tracking and debugging

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us optimize LUPO's performance and ensure fast, reliable service delivery.

  • CDN optimization
  • Caching preferences
  • Load time measurements
  • Server selection optimization

Third-Party Services

LUPO integrates with select third-party services that may set their own cookies. We carefully vet all partners to ensure they meet our privacy standards:

Content Delivery Network (CDN): We use CloudFlare to deliver our service quickly and securely worldwide. CloudFlare may set performance and security cookies.

Error Monitoring: We use privacy-focused error tracking to improve our service reliability. No personal data is shared with these services.

Analytics: We use privacy-preserving analytics that don't track individuals or create user profiles. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the LUPO application or by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies. Your continued use of LUPO after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
