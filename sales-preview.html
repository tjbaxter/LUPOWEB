Common Questions

How does LUPO know about MY company specifically?

You upload your pitch deck, case studies, battlecards, and product docs once. LUPO uses YOUR content to generate responses, not generic AI suggestions. Every response comes from YOUR approved playbook, ensuring consistency and accuracy across your entire team.

Does it work with Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet?

Yes. LUPO works with ANY video conferencing tool: Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and everything else. No integrations needed. Just start your call and LUPO listens in the background, ready to assist.

What exactly happens after the call ends?

Instantly after your call: (1) A personalized follow-up email is drafted in seconds, ready for you to review and send. (2) The full transcript is saved in your dashboard. (3) Visit the dashboard to ask the AI "What could I do better?" for instant coaching. (4) Managers see all calls with critical moments, buying signals, and team insights.

Is my call data private and secure?

Yes. All call transcripts are processed in real-time and stored encrypted. You own your data completely and can delete it anytime. We never share your call data with third parties or use it to train models for other companies.

How fast does LUPO respond during calls?

LUPO suggests responses instantly in real-time. When a prospect raises an objection, LUPO immediately provides relevant talking points from your company's playbook, so you never have to pause or fumble for the right response.
