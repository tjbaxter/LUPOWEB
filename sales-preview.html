How LUPO Works

Four simple steps to transform your sales calls

Step 1 - Setup

Upload Your Company Info

  • Upload documents or type/speak your details
  • Add your name, pricing, what you do
  • Describe problems you solve
  • AI builds your company profile instantly
  • Works with ANY video tool (Zoom, Teams, Meet)
→
Step 2 - Before Call

Set Your Call Objective

  • Speak or type 240 characters of context
  • "Following up with Anna at Oracle - price concern - closing at 50k today"
  • LUPO understands what you're trying to achieve
  • Takes 10 seconds, makes the whole call sharper
→
Step 3 - During Call

LUPO Listens & Assists

  • LUPO listens to your call
  • Detects objections & buying signals
  • Suggests responses from YOUR data
  • Responds instantly in real-time
  • Not generic AI, uses YOUR company playbook
  • Live Recap keeps you on track - shows what's happened, what's happening now, what to do next
→
Step 4 - After Call

Instant Next Steps, Summaries or Follow-Ups

  • Choose: Next steps to close the deal, call summary for CRM, or draft follow-up email
  • Full transcript saved in your dashboard
  • Visit dashboard to ask AI: "What could I do better?"
  • Review call performance with AI coaching
  • Managers see all calls with critical moments & insights
