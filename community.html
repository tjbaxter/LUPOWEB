Community Guidelines

Our community thrives on respect, collaboration, and shared learning. Here's how we keep it awesome:

🤝

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. We welcome users of all backgrounds and experience levels.

💡

Share Knowledge

Help others by sharing your experiences, tips, and solutions. Everyone benefits when we learn together.

🎯

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to LUPO and AI assistance. This helps maintain quality conversations.

🔍

Search First

Before asking questions, search previous discussions. Someone might have already found the answer.

🚫

No Spam

Avoid repetitive posts, excessive self-promotion, or off-topic content that clutters discussions.

🛡️

Report Issues

Help us maintain a safe space by reporting inappropriate content or behavior to moderators.
