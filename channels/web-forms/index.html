Channels

AI web form response and qualification

LUPO answers every form in the way the buyer actually wants: an outbound callback inside sixty seconds, or a personal email with a booking link.

How it works

1. A form is submitted. A demo request, a contact form, or a gated content download. Gated content forms count as a form channel.

2. LUPO picks the right response. The logic is channel-aware. A high-intent form triggers an outbound callback inside sixty seconds. A senior B2B buyer gets a personal email reply with a booking link instead. LUPO will never surprise-call a VP who submitted a demo form.

3. The lead is enriched and scored. Firmographics, technographics, and decision-maker title come from live company-signal data. Intent triggers such as funding, M&A, leadership hires, and headcount growth shape the score.

4. Real buyers get booked. A meeting lands on a rep's calendar. Everyone else is filtered or routed before a rep spends a minute on them.

5. Your systems stay current. The lead syncs to HubSpot, Salesforce, or any CRM, and a Slack alert lands with full context.

One qualification policy covers web form, email, chat, and phone. See how LUPO works end to end.

What gets filtered

Form fills are the noisiest channel of all. LUPO filters vendors pitching your team and tire-kickers with no budget or timeline. Support requests get routed to the right team. Wrong-fit prospects are disqualified politely.

Reps only see real buyers.

Why it matters

Speed to lead is the most studied number in inbound sales: 23% of web leads never get a response (Harvard Business Review), and a five-minute response makes contact 21 times more likely. LUPO holds a sub-2-minute median, measured in the dashboard.

Speed alone is not enough, because 73% of B2B leads are not sales-ready on arrival, per MarketingSherpa. Calling every form fill in sixty seconds just delivers junk faster. Qualification has to ride along.

That protects the scarcest resource you have: reps spend 28% of their week actually selling, according to Salesforce's State of Sales.

Common questions

How fast does LUPO respond to a form?
