Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to how we've built LUPO. Everything happens locally on your device. We can't see your screen, we don't store your data, and we never will.

Our Privacy Promise

LUPO is designed with privacy at its core. Unlike traditional AI assistants that send your data to the cloud, LUPO processes everything locally on your Mac. This means:

  • Your screen content never leaves your device
  • We don't store or have access to your queries
  • Your personal information remains completely private
  • No tracking, no analytics on your usage patterns

What We Collect

Account Information

When you create a LUPO account, we collect only the essentials:

  • Email address (for account access and updates)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • License key (to activate your subscription)

What We Don't Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Screen content or screenshots
  • Your queries or AI interactions
  • Application usage data
  • Personal files or documents
  • Browsing history or activity

Local Processing

LUPO's architecture ensures your privacy through local processing:

  • Screen analysis happens entirely on your device
  • AI responses are generated using your API key
  • No data is transmitted to our servers except for license validation
  • Screenshots are processed in memory and immediately discarded

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Stripe - For secure payment processing
  • OpenAI API - You connect directly using your own API key

These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All network communications are encrypted using TLS
  • Account passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Payment information is handled by PCI-compliant processors
  • Regular security audits of our infrastructure

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access your account information anytime
  • Update or correct your details
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your account information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll notify you of any significant changes via email.

Contact Us
