Changelog

What shipped.

LUPO moves in days, not quarters. The notable changes land here, dated and in plain language, and everything below is live.

June 2026

11 June

The homepage dashboard is the live product

The dashboard preview on lupolabs.ai now renders the real product, not a screenshot. Explore leads, intent bands, and meetings straight from the homepage.

11 June

An open ROI model

The fix, in numbers: recovered revenue, research hours returned, hires avoided. Methodology in visible text, conservative sourced defaults, every number adjustable. See the model.

11 June

Pipeline attribution in the dashboard

Deal value from the CRM, attributed to LUPO-routed leads and reported as pipeline sourced. Inbound now answers for what it turned into.

11 June

A refreshed voice lineup

New voices on the live call demo. Audition them on the homepage panel and switch between calls.

11 June

Honest comparisons, published

Six head-to-head comparison pages and an alternatives guide, sources linked throughout. Each page says plainly who the other product is for.

10 June

Dashboard KPIs revenue teams actually use

Median first response, form-to-meeting conversion, rep hours returned, and week-over-week momentum, measured on your own inbound.

9 June

The SDR morning view

The dashboard now reads the way an SDR reads the morning: leads by channel, intent bands, noise filtered out, meetings booked.

8 June

Enrichment and buying-intent scoring, live

Every inbound lead is enriched with live company-signal data (firmographics, technographics, decision-maker title) and scored for buying intent on triggers like funding, M&A, leadership hires, and headcount growth, before a rep ever sees it.

8 June

Booking inside the chat

The calendar now opens inside the conversation. Pick a slot, get the invite, never leave the chat.

7 June

Phone calls feed intent scoring

Buying signals stated on a call now score the lead, the same way signals from web forms, email, and chat do.

7 June

Channel-aware form response

High-intent web forms trigger a callback inside sixty seconds. Senior buyers get a personal email reply with a booking link instead. LUPO will never surprise-call a VP.

May 2026

28 May

LUPO's chat agent went live on lupolabs.ai

The proactive chat agent now runs on this site: it engages on landing, qualifies, and books. The same agent customers deploy on their own sites.

26 May

Microsoft 365 / Outlook booking live

LUPO books real calendar events for teams on Outlook, alongside Google Calendar.

24 May

Salesforce CRM sync live

Qualified contacts, transcripts, intent scores, and bookings written to Salesforce automatically, alongside HubSpot.

24 May

Speed to lead, measured

Time-to-first-action on every lead, tracked in the dashboard. If LUPO is slow, you see it.
