Frequently asked questions

How does LUPO work with my API keys? ⌄
LUPO provides the infrastructure and interface. You bring your own OpenAI API key for the AI functionality. This keeps your costs transparent and under your control.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ⌄
Yes! You can upgrade to Pro instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
What happens if I hit my free tier limit? ⌄
You'll see a friendly reminder to upgrade to Pro for unlimited access. Your limit resets daily at midnight.
Is my data secure? ⌄
Absolutely. LUPO processes everything locally on your device. We never see your screen content or queries.
