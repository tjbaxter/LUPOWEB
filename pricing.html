Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work? ⌄
Start with our free plan - no credit card required. You get 50 queries per day to test LUPO's capabilities. When you're ready for unlimited access, upgrade to Pro anytime.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ⌄
Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period, then you'll automatically switch back to the free plan.
What's the difference between monthly and annual billing? ⌄
Annual billing saves you 57% compared to monthly. Pay $99/year instead of $228/year. That's like getting 7 months free!
Is my data secure? ⌄
Absolutely. LUPO processes everything locally on your device. Screenshots are analyzed in real-time and never stored. Your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly share it.
Do you offer student discounts? ⌄
Yes! Students get 50% off Pro plans. Contact us at students@lupolabs.ai with your .edu email address to get your discount code.
