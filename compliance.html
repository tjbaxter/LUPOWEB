Data Handling Principles

🔐

Data Minimization

We collect and process only the minimum data necessary to provide our services, ensuring compliance with privacy-by-design principles and reducing your compliance burden.

🗂️

Purpose Limitation

All data is processed solely for the specific purposes disclosed at collection. We never use your data for undisclosed secondary purposes without explicit consent.

⏳

Retention Limits

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the stated purpose, with automatic deletion policies and regular purging of expired data to minimize compliance risks.

🔍

Transparency

We provide clear, understandable information about our data practices, including regular compliance reports and audit results available to our enterprise customers.
