The AI inbound SDR, built natively for HubSpot.

LUPO captures every inbound lead across web form, email, chat, and phone, filters the noise, scores buying intent, and books the real buyers, all inside HubSpot. It reads and writes your native objects, so the work lands where your reps already are.

Native to HubSpot, not bolted on.

LUPO connects through HubSpot's official API with OAuth. No middleware, no CSV exports, no second system to learn. It reads and writes your native objects, contacts, companies, deals, tickets, and custom objects. Lead scores and routing decisions are written straight into your HubSpot properties, so your existing workflows, views, and reports keep working exactly as they do today. LUPO fires your HubSpot workflows and responds to them, and surfaces full lead context inside the record your reps already open.

One inbound brain, every channel.

Inbound arrives everywhere at once. LUPO runs it all through a single qualification brain, so nothing leaks and no rep wastes a minute on junk.

  1. Captures every hand-raiser across web form, email, chat, and phone.
  2. Filters junk, vendor pitches, and out-of-ICP noise before it reaches a rep.
  3. Scores each lead for buying intent.
  4. Routes the real buyers to the right rep and books the meeting.
  5. Hands off with full context, every touch logged in HubSpot.

Built on HubSpot and Breeze. The inbound SDR job runs on top.

On HubSpot, LUPO builds on your HubSpot data and Breeze signals, then runs the frontline inbound job on top: resolving the leads who engage (including freemail hand-raisers), qualifying the conversation across every channel, scoring fit and intent, routing to the right rep, booking the meeting, and writing every decision back to HubSpot. Off HubSpot, the same brain runs on direct data. HubSpot data and Breeze feed LUPO; LUPO does the inbound SDR work on top of them.
