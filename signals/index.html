Common questions

Does LUPO track people who have not contacted us?

No. LUPO reads signals on the buyers who come to you across web form, email, chat, and phone. It does not monitor strangers, scrape social profiles, or build cold prospect lists. This is inbound, not outbound.

Where do the company signals come from?

From live company-signal data: funding, mergers and acquisitions, leadership hires, and headcount growth on the account a lead belongs to. LUPO enriches the company behind the lead, then scores fit and intent.

Can LUPO act on a signal automatically?

Yes. LUPO scores the signal, routes the lead to the right rep, books the real buyers, and writes every decision back to the CRM with the reasoning attached. You decide how far it goes.

Does it work with HubSpot?

Yes. LUPO is HubSpot-native: every signal, score, and routing decision is written to the HubSpot record. It also works with Salesforce natively, or any CRM reachable by webhook.
