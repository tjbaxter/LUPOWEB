Common questions

Why don't you publish a sticker price?

Because the right number depends on inbound volume, channels covered, and which integrations matter to you. Pricing per pilot is honest about that — and it's how every early-stage SaaS company in this space starts. Once we have density in each segment we'll publish self-serve pricing.

What's the difference between SMB and Enterprise, really?

Same agentic brain on both. The Enterprise tier surfaces capabilities that only matter when you have a sales team to route to — round-robin assignment, CRM sync, vendor-spam filtering at scale, custom workflow integrations. The SMB tier strips those out and focuses on the owner-operator's actual problem: never miss a call, book the appointment, send the confirmation.

Will LUPO annoy senior buyers with a robot call?

No. LUPO is channel-aware. A senior B2B buyer who fills out a demo form gets a personal email with a booking link — not an unexpected outbound call thirty seconds after submitting. Phone callbacks fire only on consumer forms, inbound calls, or chats where the visitor has explicitly asked to be called.

Is this just an AI receptionist? An AI SDR?

Neither in isolation. LUPO is the qualification layer that captures every inbound across all four channels and routes the qualified ones into your existing tools. Voice-only AI receptionists (Air, PolyAI, Rosie) handle the phone and stop there. Email-only AI SDRs (11x, Artisan, Regie) do outbound. LUPO does inbound across phone, email, web form, and chat — with one shared brain.

How long until it's set up?

Most pilots go live inside a week of the first call. Onboarding is hands-on — qualification criteria, routing rules, calendar handling, escalation thresholds, and approval gates are configured against your real inbound before anything goes live.

What about security and compliance?

Encryption in transit and controlled access to customer data. Conversations are never used to train external AI models. A signed Data Processing Agreement is available on request. Formal SOC 2 / HIPAA attestations will be sought as the product matures and design partners require them.
