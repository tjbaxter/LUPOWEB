Common questions

Why don't you publish a sticker price?

The right number depends on inbound volume, channels covered, and which integrations matter to you. Pilots typically run $3–$15k per month, agreed upfront, with the success metric written into the contract. No hidden tiers, no usage surprises.

How does LUPO follow up with leads?

Email first. A senior buyer who fills out a demo form gets a personal email with a booking link in minutes, from your own domain. LUPO answers inbound calls live and continues chat conversations in chat, but it only places an outbound call when the lead has explicitly asked for one. Channel-aware by design.

Is this an AI receptionist or an AI SDR?

LUPO is the AI inbound SDR. Voice-only AI receptionists handle the phone and stop there. Most AI SDRs do outbound. LUPO runs the inbound side end to end: it captures every lead across phone, email, web form, and chat, qualifies it, and routes real buyers into your existing tools. One shared brain across every channel.

How long until it's set up?

Plan for four to eight weeks to fully live, depending on scope. Routing rules, CRM integration, calendar handoffs, escalation thresholds, and qualification criteria are all configured against your real inbound before launch. Most pilots start phased: one channel goes live first, then the rest are added in sequence rather than all at once.

What happens if LUPO doesn't hit the success metric?

The pilot ends. No conversion to an annual contract, no upsell pressure. Before kickoff we agree on one measurable metric tied to your funnel and write it into the pilot contract. Examples we've used: "sub-2-minute response on every inbound during business hours", "N qualified meetings booked per week", or "X% of vendor pitches correctly filtered before a rep sees them". You know what success means before signing, no surprises at month one.

What about security and compliance?

Encryption in transit and controlled access to customer data. Conversations are never used to train external AI models. A signed Data Processing Agreement is available on request. Formal SOC 2 readiness will be pursued as the product matures and design partners require it.
